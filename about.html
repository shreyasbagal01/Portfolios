About

Co-founder of Webbloom Digital, a Pune-based digital marketing agency. Three years building organic and paid growth systems for brands that needed to be taken seriously online — clinics, real-estate developers, professional services firms, and founders.

Quick answer
Shreyas Bagal is the co-founder of Webbloom Digital, a Pune-based digital marketing agency. Across three years he has delivered 1.1M+ verified reach for clients, run 20 Meta Ads campaigns with a ₹58 blended CPL, and grown his own LinkedIn from cold to 748,571 impressions in 12 months. The short version

I run Webbloom Digital with a small, focused team out of Pune. We take on a deliberately limited number of clients each quarter and we do work we can defend with screenshots. Most of what we do is Meta Ads, social media, SEO, reels production, and personal branding — for brands where the founder, clinician, or owner is willing to be part of the work.

The slightly longer version

I started doing digital marketing in college, freelancing while I finished a BE in Computer Engineering at TSSM's BSCOER, Pune (2021–2025). Graduated 4th rank with a CGPA of 8.98. By the end of college I had enough client work to skip the placement process and co-found Webbloom Digital.

The thing I keep returning to is this: most agencies sell activity. The right agency sells outcomes. Outcomes mean leads in your CRM, calls on your phone, footfalls in your clinic, enquiries on your sales team's WhatsApp. Everything else is theatre. Webbloom is built around that distinction.

What I believe about marketing

  1. Specificity beats clever. Named entities, hard numbers, real war stories — every time.
  2. Three lanes, ruthlessly held. Most brands dilute themselves trying to do five.
  3. Screenshots or it didn't happen. Every number on this site is verifiable inside the platform-native dashboard.
  4. Personal branding is now a B2B channel. Founders who show up online win the business their hidden peers do not.
  5. Geo-lock before you go broad. For Pune, single-suburb targeting beats Pune-wide every time.
  6. AI search matters as much as Google. If you are not in ChatGPT and Perplexity, you are invisible to a growing slice of buyers.

How I work with clients

Webbloom takes on a focused number of clients per quarter — usually six to ten across the agency. We hold a hard cap because the work cannot be done well at high volume. Engagements typically run 6 months minimum so the system has time to compound. We sign mutual NDAs gladly. We share Meta Ads Manager screenshots and platform-native analytics with every monthly report. Everything we produce belongs to the client — no platform lock-in.

Frequently asked

How big is Webbloom Digital?

Small and focused by design. The core team is six people across strategy, content, design, video editing, and account management. We deliberately cap our client count so quality stays high.

Who do you work with?

Clinics, real-estate developers, professional services firms, founders, and select consumer brands in and around Pune. We take fit calls with anyone outside that list but rarely accept them — the work is sharper when we stay close to the categories we know well.

Are you hiring?

We hire deliberately. If you are an exceptional content writer, video editor, or paid-media operator based in Pune, send us a note via the contact page with examples of your work. We open one or two roles a year.

Can I work with you remotely?

Yes, with a preference for Pune-based clients. Roughly 80% of our active clients are within 15 km of our office; the remaining 20% are in Mumbai, Bangalore, or NRI-led businesses.
