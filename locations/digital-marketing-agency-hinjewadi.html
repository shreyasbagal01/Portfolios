Why Hinjewadi needs a different playbook

The IT-corridor audience is the most digital-native in Pune. They research everything before buying — clinics, schools, gyms, properties, software. They will not call you back; they will WhatsApp you, or fill a form. A Hinjewadi marketing engagement has to assume the buyer is on the phone, comparison-shopping you against three other options, while they are still in the office.

Hinjewadi audience signals

  • LinkedIn is the dominant platform for any B2B sale here — more than Instagram, more than Google Ads
  • English-led creative outperforms regional creative for the IT workforce; reverses for older family-decision buys
  • Form-fill conversion windows are tighter — leads cool in 4–6 hours if you do not WhatsApp them
  • Real-estate buying is heavily online — virtual tours, WhatsApp first, site visit second
  • Premium pricing converts well; bargain-led creative underperforms

— Questions

Frequently asked

Do you work with B2B SaaS startups in Hinjewadi?

Yes, specifically with founder-led B2B SaaS where LinkedIn personal branding can carry significant pipeline. We have built that playbook on our own founder profile (748,571 LinkedIn impressions in 12 months) and apply it on client accounts.

Can you target enterprise IT buyers via LinkedIn?

Yes — through founder personal branding plus, where budget allows, paid LinkedIn ads. We typically lead with organic personal branding because the ROI is better for early-stage SaaS. Paid LinkedIn comes in once organic is producing baseline pipeline.

Do you handle Tathawade real-estate projects?

Yes. Tathawade is one of our active geographies. The same playbook used on our 8,005-lead Charholi campaign adapts to Tathawade — geo-locked targeting, three-variant creative testing, lead-form objective, disqualifying questions inside the form.

Do I need both LinkedIn and Meta Ads in Hinjewadi?

Depends on the business. Pure B2B SaaS: usually LinkedIn-only is enough for the first 6 months. Consumer/local services: Meta Ads + Instagram. Hybrid (e.g. a B2B service that also sells to local SMBs): both.

What's the smallest engagement you take in the Hinjewadi corridor?

We take focused engagements starting around ₹35,000/month. The Hinjewadi market rewards quality over volume — we don't do high-volume, low-touch work here.
