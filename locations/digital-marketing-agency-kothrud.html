Why Kothrud needs a different playbook

Kothrud is not Baner. It is not Hadapsar. The audience here is one of the most established Marathi-speaking neighbourhoods in Pune, with strong family decision-making, a high concentration of healthcare and education buyers, and meaningful word-of-mouth on top of digital. National agencies templating Mumbai or Bangalore strategies miss these things.

The Kothrud buyer journey

Most Kothrud buyers research before they enquire. For a clinic, that means a Google Business Profile with 30+ reviews, photos that look like the actual practice, and an Instagram account that does not look generic. For a school, it means a parent-credible Instagram + a Meta Ads campaign that runs across the December–February admissions window, not year-round at the same intensity.

Can you visit my Kothrud office for kick-off?

How much does digital marketing cost in Kothrud?

Depends on scope. A focused Meta Ads engagement starts around ₹25,000/month retainer plus your ad spend. Full-stack (Meta + social + reels) sits in the ₹70,000–₹1,50,000/month range. We share itemised quotes on the first call — no surprises.

Do you work with small Kothrud businesses or only big brands?

Both, but we have a deliberate cap on client count so the work stays focused. We take on roughly 6–10 active clients at a time across the agency.

Will my campaigns target Kothrud or all of Pune?

Geo-locked to the right radius for your business. A clinic on Karve Road typically wants a 3–5 km radius. A school typically wants 6–8 km. A jeweller might want 10+ km. The targeting is set after a first-call diagnostic.
