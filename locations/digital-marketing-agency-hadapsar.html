Why Hadapsar needs a different playbook

The Hadapsar audience tilts younger, more dual-income, more comfortable with online-first interactions, and more responsive to short-form video than older Pune neighbourhoods. They also have less time. The campaigns that work here are tighter, sharper, and more visually polished than the ones that win in (say) Sadashiv Peth.
