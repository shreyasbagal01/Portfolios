How the first call works

  1. You book a 30-minute slot.
  2. Before the call, I look at your current Meta Ads (if any), Instagram, LinkedIn, and Google Business Profile.
  3. On the call, I share what I found, what I would change in the next 90 days, and a rough budget range for the work.
  4. If it is a fit, we send a written proposal within 48 hours. If it is not, I refer you to a better-fit agency or freelancer.

No sales pressure, no decks, no "we are excited to partner with you" emails. Just one call, an honest read, and a clear next step.

Frequently asked

Is the first call free?

Yes. The first 30-minute call is free and ends with a clear recommendation — work with us, or work with someone else. No catch.

What time zones do you take calls?

IST working hours, Monday to Saturday, 10:00–19:00. We will accommodate Mumbai / Bangalore / Delhi schedules easily, and NRI clients on weekend slots.

How quickly can you start?

If we have capacity, work begins within 7 days of a signed contract. We always check capacity on the first call before discussing timelines.

Do you work with very small businesses?

Yes, with a minimum monthly commitment. We will tell you on the call whether your business size fits our pricing — and recommend alternatives if it doesn't.

What if I am not in Pune?

We accept select Mumbai, Bangalore, and Delhi clients. NRI-founded Indian businesses are also welcome. Outside India we usually decline — different time zone, different creative cues, different cost dynamics.
