Silver Horse Ventures: #59 → top 5 in 60 days.

How a Pune venture firm climbed from page-six obscurity to local-pack visibility in two months — and landed the #1 Reddit post in its category inside 24 hours.

The starting point

Silver Horse Ventures is a Pune-based venture firm with a clean reputation in their network but minimal organic visibility. On the query that mattered most — their primary service category, Pune-localised — they sat at local rank #59. That meant they showed up nowhere in the search journey of their potential prospects. The brief: get into the local pack, in two months, without any paid promotion.

What we changed

1. Google Business Profile rebuild

The profile was incomplete — wrong category, missing services, no products, no posts, only four photos, no Q&A activity. Week one:

  • Primary category corrected (single most impactful change)
  • Three secondary categories added
  • Full services list populated with descriptions
  • 20+ photos uploaded across exterior, interior, team, work moments
  • Weekly Posts started — service updates, insights, milestones
  • Q&A section seeded with the 8 most common prospect questions

2. Review acquisition

From 11 reviews to 34 inside six weeks, all with prospect-relevant keywords occurring naturally. Review responses written in a voice that matched the firm. Local SEO rewards review velocity almost as much as review count.

3. Three-stack schema markup

The website was missing schema entirely. We deployed:

  • LocalBusiness with geo coordinates, opening hours, address, telephone, sameAs links
  • Organization with founder, areaServed, knowsAbout
  • FAQPage covering 10 prospect-intent questions
  • BreadcrumbList on every page

4. Brand mention building

For local SEO and AI search, brand mentions correlate more strongly with visibility than backlinks. We placed Silver Horse mentions in:

  • A guest post on a Pune business publication
  • Two Quora answers responding to relevant questions
  • One Reddit post in r/Pune that hit #1 in 24 hours by leading with a useful, non-promotional insight (the firm got a mention near the close)
  • A LinkedIn long-form piece syndicated to the founder's account

5. llms.txt & AI search coverage

We generated llms.txt and llms-full.txt files, opened robots.txt to GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, OAI-SearchBot, and Google-Extended. Within four weeks Silver Horse was being cited in Perplexity answers for category-relevant Pune queries — the AI search side of the move.

"For local SEO in Pune, your Google Business Profile is doing more work than your website. Most agencies are still operating on the opposite assumption."

The Reddit post that worked

One of the most asked questions in this engagement: "How did the Reddit post hit #1?" The answer is boring — the post was useful first, promotional last. It opened with a contrarian insight backed by data, ran through three points, and only mentioned Silver Horse in the third paragraph as the source of a referenced number. r/Pune upvoted it because it was actually helpful. The mention near the end did its work without triggering the community's spam allergy.

Lessons portable to other Pune local businesses

  1. Google Business Profile first, website second. Most local-SEO wins live in GBP — fix it before you touch HTML.
  2. Schema markup is a free moat. If your competitors are not running it, you can leapfrog them on technical merit alone.
  3. Brand mentions > backlinks for local + AI search. Reddit, Quora, YouTube, Wikipedia (where eligible) all matter.
  4. Review velocity matters as much as count. 10 reviews this month outranks 50 reviews last year.
  5. Open the door for AI crawlers. Robots.txt is now an SEO control surface, not just a politeness file.
