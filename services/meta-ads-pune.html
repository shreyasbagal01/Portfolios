What we actually do on Meta Ads

Most Meta Ads agencies in Pune sell "campaign setup" and stop there. We treat Meta Ads as a system: every campaign is one of three variants, every variant has a kill rule, and every Monday we cut what isn't working. That discipline is what produces a ₹58 blended CPL in a city where most clients pay ₹150–₹250.

Audience research first

Before any ad runs, we map the buyer. For a real-estate project we look at six things: the suburb's pin codes, average flat-size searches in those pin codes, competitor ad libraries, the project's USP, the buyer's likely budget band, and the seasonality of property enquiries. Only after that do we open Ads Manager.

3-variant creative testing

Every launch ships with three creative variants — usually one image-led, one video-led, and one carousel. We give each 72 hours and a spend threshold. The bottom two are cut by Friday. The 8,005-lead Charholi campaign is the third creative iteration of a campaign that started with two losers.

Lead-form objective, not click-through

Every campaign in our dataset is a Meta Lead Form objective, not a website click. Removing the landing-page step is what makes ₹24–₹50 CPLs possible in real estate. The trade-off is lead quality — which is why we layer on the next thing.

Disqualifying questions inside the form

Each form carries one or two purchase-intent qualifiers — budget range, decision timeline, possession year preference. Pre-filtered leads convert roughly 3× better at the sales-call stage than open forms. The CPL goes up slightly; cost-per-meeting drops sharply.

Weekly performance reviews

We share a one-page WhatsApp report every Monday morning: leads delivered, CPL, the variant that won, the variant we're killing, and the change we are testing next. No PowerPoint, no jargon, no "engagement rate" theatre.

"Volume without efficiency drains budget. Efficiency without volume doesn't move the business. The campaigns that hit both lists are the ones we scaled."

Frequently asked

What's a typical cost per lead on Meta Ads in Pune?

On our managed accounts the blended cost per lead sits at ₹58.33 across 20 real-estate campaigns. The best campaign hit ₹24.68 per lead. For most Pune real-estate, clinics, and service businesses, a ₹40–₹150 CPL is realistic with disciplined creative testing and geo-locked targeting. Agencies quoting ₹200+ CPL on real estate are usually running broad audiences and image-only creative.

How long does it take Meta Ads to start producing leads?

First leads usually arrive within 24–48 hours of launch. Stable CPL takes 7–14 days because the algorithm needs spend to optimise. We commit to a clear cost-per-lead target by day 14 — if we are not on track, we pause and rebuild the creative.

Do I pay Webbloom for the ad spend too?

No. You pay Meta directly through your own ad account. We never mark up ad spend or take a percentage of it. Our charge is a fixed monthly management retainer, separate from your budget.

Will the leads be in Pune only?

Yes, by default. Every campaign we run is geo-locked to specific Pune pin codes or neighbourhoods — Charholi, Moshi, Pimple Saudagar, Ravet, Tathawade, Wakad, Hadapsar, Kothrud, Hinjewadi, and others depending on your business. Single-suburb targeting consistently outperforms Pune-wide campaigns in our data.

What if my product is not real estate?

We have run Meta Ads for healthcare (742,806 verified Instagram views for Painex Clinic in 3 months), personal branding, and local service businesses. The same playbook — geo-lock, 3-variant testing, lead-form objective, disqualifying questions — applies. The creative and qualifiers change.

Do you also do Google Ads?

Meta Ads is our specialty. We will refer you to a trusted Google Ads partner if that is the better fit, or run a paired strategy where we handle Meta and the partner handles Search.

Can I see the actual Meta Ads Manager screenshots?

Yes. Every number on our results page is verifiable inside Meta Ads Manager. We share screenshots on the first call when you are evaluating us.

Do you work with businesses outside Pune?

We currently focus on Pune and Pimpri-Chinchwad clients because local creative cues are a major lever for us. If you are in Mumbai or Bangalore we will discuss it on a call and refer if it is a poor fit.
