Our Reels playbook

1. Hook in 1.2 seconds

If the first 1.2 seconds do not pattern-interrupt, the reel is dead. We write the hook before the script. Hooks come in five shapes that consistently work in Pune feeds: a sharp contrarian claim, a named-entity reveal, a stat with a comma in it ("742,806"), a question the viewer is already asking, or a body-language pattern interrupt (looking off-frame, sudden gesture, mid-action freeze).

2. Edit cadence — cut every 1.8 seconds

The average Pune reel sits on a single shot for too long. Our default cadence is a cut every 1.8 seconds, with B-roll or zoom-in/zoom-out between speaker beats. Watch-through climbs sharply once the cadence is right.

3. Captions burned into the frame

Roughly 80% of Instagram viewers scroll with sound off. We caption every line, in a brand-consistent font, animated on/off in time with speech. Marathi-Hindi-English code-switching gets handled cleanly because we live in Pune and write the captions ourselves.

4. The "quotable" closing line

Every reel ends on one line built to be quoted back at us in the comments. That comment-quote is the engagement engine — it drives the algorithm to push the reel into more feeds. The 1,092 comments on our founder profile in 12 months came largely from this single tactic.

5. Three lanes, not ten formats

For each brand we pick three content lanes the reels can sit inside — for a clinic, that might be (a) condition explainers, (b) day-in-the-life, (c) patient stories. Three lanes hit consistency without boredom. We refuse a fourth.

"Three posts did 54% of the year's work. The other 46% came from sustaining a baseline the breakouts could amplify."

What a typical month looks like

  • One shoot day — usually at your premises, 4–6 hours, all the raw footage for the month captured in one block
  • 12–16 reels delivered — depending on length, complexity, and platform mix
  • Multi-platform repurposing — every reel ships to Instagram, YouTube Shorts, and (where it fits) LinkedIn video
  • Weekly performance review — which reel performed, why, and what we are changing for next week

Who this is for

Reels work disproportionately well for businesses where the founder, doctor, lawyer, or owner can speak on camera. If you (or your spokesperson) cannot do 4 hours on camera once a month, reels will struggle. We will tell you that on the first call — and recommend a stills-led Instagram strategy instead.

Pricing

Frequently asked

How many reels can I expect per month?

12 to 16 per month is the standard cadence — enough volume to give the algorithm consistent signal without diluting quality. Pricing scales with reel count and complexity (one-shot talking head vs. multi-cut B-roll-heavy).

Do you write the script or do I?

We write it. You provide the expertise — we sit with you for 30 minutes on Zoom to extract the angles, then turn them into scripts you approve before shoot day.

Can a Pune-based small clinic actually hit 200K views?

Yes, with disciplined hook writing and the right content lanes. Painex Clinic, a Pune pain clinic, hit 742,806 verified Instagram views in three months — 98.9% from people who weren't already followers. The unlock is treating reels as research, not broadcast.

What about Marathi-language reels?

We produce reels in Marathi, Hindi, and English — and code-switched, which is how most of Pune actually talks. Multilingual hooks have driven major performance for our healthcare clients.

Do you provide the editor or do I need to hire one?

We provide the full team — strategy, scriptwriting, shoot direction (us behind the camera or coaching yours), and CapCut/Adobe editing. You provide yourself or your spokesperson, and a quiet room with light.

How do you choose hooks?

We A/B test hook concepts in batches — usually three variants per reel theme. Within 3–4 weeks we have a data-backed library of hook shapes that work for your specific audience. Then we ride them.

Will you post the reels too or just deliver them?

Both options. On a full social retainer we post and manage community. On a reels-only retainer we deliver the files and you publish. Most clients prefer full retainer.

Got a face for camera and nothing to show for it?

30-minute call. Tell us your category, your spokesperson availability, and the goal. We will tell you whether reels are the move and what your first month should look like.
