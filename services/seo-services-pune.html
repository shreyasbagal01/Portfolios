What we do under "SEO"

The phrase "SEO" hides a lot of distinct work. Here is exactly what we cover on a Webbloom retainer.

1. Technical SEO

Core Web Vitals (LCP, CLS, INP), HTTPS hygiene, mobile responsiveness, canonical tags, hreflang where relevant, server-side rendering audits, sitemap generation, robots.txt and llms.txt curation, schema markup (LocalBusiness, FAQPage, Article, Service, BreadcrumbList, Person, ProfilePage). If Google or an AI crawler cannot read your site cleanly, nothing else matters.

2. Local SEO & Google Business Profile

For Pune businesses with a physical address, the highest-leverage SEO surface is not your website — it is your Google Business Profile. We optimise category selection, attributes, services, products, posts, photo strategy, review acquisition, and review responses. This is how Silver Horse Ventures went from local-rank #59 to top 5 in 60 days.

3. Content & on-page SEO

We write pages targeted at specific buyer queries, structured for both Google featured snippets and AI citation — short, fact-rich passages around 134–167 words, clean H-tag hierarchy, direct answer in the opening paragraph, supporting evidence below. Our blog content gets cited by Perplexity within weeks of publishing because of this structure.

4. GEO — Generative Engine Optimisation

This is the new layer most Pune SEO agencies ignore. GEO covers everything that makes a page citable by AI: llms.txt files for site-wide context, llms-full.txt for deep content, schema markup beyond the basics, entity-rich author bios, fact-dense paragraphs, and AI crawler access in robots.txt (allowing GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended, OAI-SearchBot). If you do not appear in ChatGPT and Perplexity answers, you are invisible to a growing slice of your buyers.

5. Off-page & brand mentions

For AI search engines, brand mentions correlate 3× more strongly with citation than backlinks. We build mentions across Reddit, YouTube, LinkedIn, Quora, Wikipedia stubs (where eligible), and trade press. The goal is for your brand name to appear next to the categories you sell.

Frequently asked

How long does SEO take to show results in Pune?

For local SEO (Google Business Profile), expect visible Local Pack movement in 30–60 days. For organic content ranking, expect 90–180 days for competitive Pune keywords, faster for long-tail queries. AI search citations (ChatGPT, Perplexity) can start appearing in 4–8 weeks once llms.txt and schema are in place.

What is GEO and how is it different from SEO?

GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) is the practice of optimising for AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Overviews — surfaces that summarise answers rather than show ten blue links. GEO overlaps with SEO but adds: llms.txt files, fact-dense citable paragraphs, allowing AI crawlers in robots.txt, entity-rich author bios, and brand mentions across Reddit / YouTube / Quora. Brand mentions correlate 3× more with AI citations than backlinks do.

Can you rank my Pune business on Google for the top keywords?

Possibly — depends on the keyword competitiveness and your current state. We will tell you honestly after a free audit. We took Silver Horse Ventures from local rank #59 to top 5 in 60 days on relevant queries. For very competitive Pune-wide queries (e.g. 'digital marketing agency Pune'), expect 6–12 months of consistent work.

Should I rank in ChatGPT and Perplexity if Google still drives my traffic?

Yes. AI-referred traffic is growing roughly 527% year-over-year and converts about 4.4× higher than organic Google clicks. Only 23% of marketers are investing in GEO right now — which means low competition and a window to plant your flag before everyone else does.

Do you do link building?

Selectively, and we never buy links. We focus on brand-mention placement (Reddit threads, YouTube features, podcast appearances, guest posts in genuine trade publications) which now matters more for AI search than backlinks do for Google.

What is llms.txt and do I need one?

llms.txt is a plain-text file at the root of your site (yourdomain.com/llms.txt) that gives AI crawlers a summary of who you are, what you offer, and where the important content lives. It is the AI equivalent of an XML sitemap. We generate llms.txt and llms-full.txt for every SEO client — yes, you need one.

Can I do SEO myself with templates?

For very small businesses with a tight budget — yes, do the basics yourself. Complete your Google Business Profile, write one strong page per service, add FAQ schema, and answer reviews. For competitive industries (real estate, healthcare, legal in Pune), the work compounds enough that hiring is worth it within 6 months.
